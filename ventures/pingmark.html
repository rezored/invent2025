The Pingmark Protocol

A Universal Semantic Identifier for Physical Locations.

science

Abstract

Current geolocation standards (GPS coordinates, Plus Codes) are designed for machines, not humans. They lack semantic context. Pingmark introduces a textual standard starting with the !@ prefix (e.g., !@starbucks.union-sq) to allow humans to share precise location context across platforms.

Core Concept

Unlike DNS, Pingmark depends on a decentralized registry of spatial anchors.

!@brand.location.sub-location
!@hotel-hilton.lobby.desk-2

Applications

  • Logistics: Precise drop-off points beyond street addresses.
  • Emergency: Rapid semantic sharing of location context.
  • AR/VR: Anchoring digital content to physical semantic zones.
